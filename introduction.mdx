## What you can build

- **Accept payments** via ecommerce and in-person channels
- **Route transactions** intelligently across acquirers with Smart Routing
- **Manage risk** with configurable rule-based risk controls
- **Move money** with payout flows including OTP verification
- **Reconcile** with detailed settlement reports and ledger entries
