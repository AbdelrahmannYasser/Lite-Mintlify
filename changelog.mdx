# Changelog

## v1 — Current

Initial public release of the Lite Dashboard API.

**Available APIs:**
- 3DS Authentication
- Merchant Configuration
- User Management
- Smart Routing (Profiles, Rules, Facts)
- Terminal Service
- Risk Rules
- Payment Reports
- Payment Configurations
- Channels (Ecommerce & In-Person)
- Money Movement (Payouts & Beneficiaries)
- Pricing Service
- Ledger (Accounts & Journal Entries)
- Settlement Reports
- Email Service
- Business Management
